Tutorials 🎬

Short clips showing Capy's little tricks — more on the way! 🌿

1 · Pick your languages

Choose what you're learning and the language you already know — add as many pairs as you like.

2 · Add a word by typing

Type any word and Capy adds the meaning, examples, pronunciation, and variations.

3 · Review with flashcards

Tap to reveal, hear it, and rate how well you know it — tricky words come back more often.

4 · Add words from any app

Select text in Safari — or anywhere — and share it straight into Word Review.

5 · Add a word from a photo

Share a picture and Capy reads the word right off it.

6 · Grab many words from a screenshot

Snap a screenshot of a page and add every word it finds in one go.

7 · Play: Capy Quiz

Turn your saved words into a quiz and win oranges 🍊 with Capy.

8 · Play: Capy Match

Race the clock matching words to their meanings.

9 · Make it yours

Themes, daily reminders, your own AI key, and tools to organize your word bank.
