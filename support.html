Common questions

How do I add words?

On the Add screen you can type a word, speak it, or take/choose a photo (OCR). You can also share text or an image from another app straight into Word Review using the iOS share sheet.

What do the subscriptions unlock?

Word Review is free to start. Plus and Pro raise your monthly AI enrichment limits and unlock photo word capture (OCR). Bring Your Own Key is a one-time unlock that lets you use your own AI provider key (Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, or Grok).

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions, to change or cancel.

Where is my data stored?

Your words are stored on your device. They're only sent to our secure backend when you ask for AI enrichment, and we don't store your vocabulary on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
