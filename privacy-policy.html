Privacy Policy

Word Review · Capybara Around The World
Effective date: June 16, 2026

Word Review is a vocabulary learning app that helps you capture and review words and phrases you encounter in daily life. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. What data we collect

Vocabulary content you add. Words, phrases, notes, and images you enter into the app are stored locally on your device. They are only sent to our servers when you explicitly request AI-powered enrichment (examples, pronunciation tips, translations).

App preferences and settings. Your language preferences, theme selection, review streak, and app settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (NSUserDefaults / Keychain). They never leave your device.

In-app purchase status. If you subscribe or make a purchase, Apple processes the transaction. We receive only a confirmation of entitlement status — no payment details, card numbers, or billing information are ever sent to us.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, browsing history, or any device identifiers for tracking purposes.

2. How we use your data

When you request AI enrichment for a vocabulary item, the word or phrase is sent to our secure backend (a Supabase Edge Function). This is used solely to generate example sentences, pronunciation notes, and contextual tips in your chosen language. We do not store your vocabulary on our servers, log the content of AI requests beyond what is needed for error diagnosis, or use your vocabulary to train any model.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third-party data brokers.

3. Third-party services

Google Gemini API — Used to generate vocabulary enrichment (examples, tips). Requests are proxied through our backend; your data is subject to Google's API Terms of Service.

Google Cloud Text-to-Speech — Used to pronounce vocabulary items aloud. Audio is cached locally on your device after the first request. Subject to Google Cloud Terms of Service.

Apple In-App Purchase / StoreKit — Handles all payment processing. We never see your payment details. Subject to Apple's standard EULA.

Supabase — Hosts our backend proxy for AI requests. Requests are not logged or stored beyond transient processing. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

4. Data storage and security

All vocabulary, settings, and review history are stored locally on your device. Sensitive data such as your own API keys (if you use the Bring Your Own Key feature) are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the operating system.

We do not operate user accounts or a database of your personal vocabulary. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

5. Children's privacy

Word Review is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your rights and choices

Delete your data. You can delete individual vocabulary items or entire language profiles from within the app at any time. Deleting the app removes all local data permanently.

Export your data. The app includes a full export feature (JSON and CSV) so you can take your vocabulary with you at any time.

Opt out of AI features. All AI enrichment features are opt-in and require an active internet connection. You can use the app entirely offline for review without any data leaving your device.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
